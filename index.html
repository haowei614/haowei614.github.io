// 01. research interests

What I Work On

My work connects requirements engineering, software reliability, and applied AI methods for practical smart software systems.

AI

Generative AI for Requirements Engineering

Applying large language models to support requirements engineering workflows, including specification analysis, consistency checking, and structured scenario generation.

SE

Software Reliability

Enhancing reliability in smart software and systems through AI-assisted requirements engineering and practical validation methods.

SP

Deep-Fake Speech Detection

Studying deep-fake speech detection with features inspired by the human auditory system and robust modeling for realistic spoofing scenarios.

KG

Knowledge Graphs & ML

Exploring knowledge graph and machine learning methods as a complementary line of research, building on prior work from JAIST collaborations.
