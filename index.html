Haowei Cheng

Shinjuku, Tokyo, Japan

I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science and Communications Engineering, Waseda University in Tokyo, Japan. I am supervised by Prof. Hironori Washizaki. My Ph.D. study focuses on software engineering and AI, specifically on enhancing reliability in smart software and systems through Generative AI-Driven requirements engineering.

I obtained my M.S. degree from Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology(JAIST), under the guidance of Prof.Masashi Unoki. My research topic was deep-fake speech detection with a focus on leveraging insights from the human auditory system. Additionally, under the guidance of Junior Associate Professor Teeradaj Racharak, I completed minor research related to knowledge graphs and machine learning.
